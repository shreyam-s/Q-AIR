Health and Safety

How Air Quality Affects Your Health

Prolonged exposure to high pollution can lead to serious health issues such as asthma, cardiovascular problems, and long-term respiratory diseases.

Health Tips During High AQI Days

  • Stay indoors during peak pollution hours.
  • Use air purifiers in your home.
  • Wear N95 masks if going outside.
